User Manual

Welcome to the Patient Hand Gesture Recognition Application!

In this application, you can use your hand gestures to communicate with your family or healthcare provider, this application makes it easy to do so using natural hand movements.

How to Use the Application

Using the application is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Open the application on your computer or device.
  2. Allow the application to access your camera.
  3. Make a hand gesture in front of the camera.
  4. The application will recognize the gesture and take the corresponding action.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing problems with the application, try the following:

  1. Make sure that the application has permission to access your camera.
  2. Make sure that the lighting in the room is sufficient for the camera to capture the hand gestures clearly.
  3. Try making the hand gestures slower and more exaggerated to ensure that they are clearly captured by the camera.
  4. If you are still having problems, try restarting the application or refreshing the page.
